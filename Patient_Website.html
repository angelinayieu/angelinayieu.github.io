Frequently Asked Questions

Will this replace my doctor's advice?

No. This is a supplement to clinical care, not a replacement. Always follow your oncologist's guidance. We're adding lifestyle insights, not changing your treatment.

How much time does it take?

As little as 5 minutes per week for the check-in. Connecting your patient portal or wearables takes about 5 minutes once, then it's automatic.

What if I miss a week?

No problem! Partial data still helps. Life during chemo is unpredictable ‚Äî we completely understand. There's no penalty for missing check-ins.

Is this a clinical trial?

No. This is observational research. We're not testing a new drug or asking you to change your treatment. We're just learning from your experience.

When will I see my personalized insights?

You'll get a basic personal summary within 4-6 weeks of consistent data. More detailed insights develop as the model learns from more participants.

Can my caregiver help?

Yes! Caregivers can help with check-ins and data entry. We also track caregiver burden as part of the research ‚Äî you're both important.

Who can participate?

Anyone 18+ who is currently receiving chemotherapy, or who finished chemotherapy within the past 2 years. Any cancer type.
